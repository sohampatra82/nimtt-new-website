Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for applying to School Level Courses?

One who completed 8th class from any Govt. Recognized board and his/her minimum age is over 14 is eligible for 10th. One who completed 10th class from any Govt. Recognized board is eligible for 12th class in any stream like Arts, Commerce, Science. We have additional Vocational subjects in both 10th & 12th class level.

Is there any opportunity for students to take a chance of lateral entry after any diploma course?

Yes. For some professional courses like Engineering, Paramedical, Pharmacy, lateral entry after diploma courses is acceptable by the universities. In that case, your course duration will decrease by 1 year.

From which universities are the courses done in your institute?

There are many renowned and UGC, AIU, NAAC, BCI, PCI, AICTE, NCTE approved universities from where we are offering our courses in different departments. They are Kalinga University, Mahakaushal University, Mangalayatan University, Arunachal University of Studies, Vinayaka Global University, Jaipur National University, Sabarmati University, NIILM University, Glocal University, Asian International University, North East Christian University, Sikkim Professional University, Bir Tikendrajit University, etc.

From which universities are the Distance and Online courses done in your institute?

There are many renowned and UGC, DEB, AIU, NAAC, AICTE approved universities from where we are offering our distance and online courses in selective departments. They are Mangalayatan University, Jaipur National University, Vivekanand Global University, CSJM University, Swami Vivekananda Subharti University, and Suresh Gyan Vihar University.

What is the process of admission?

First, we collect the educational documents from the students. Then, as per the student’s requirements, we send their documents to the suggestive universities of their course for approval. Once confirmation comes from the university, we inform the students to come to our office or complete the admission process online.

What do students get after admission?

After admission, students get a welcome mail from our student support team, their course syllabus in detail, and the Fees Payment Receipt. Then we inform students of their university updates accordingly.

How do you suggest people take admission in professional courses?

We have over 20 years of experience counseling students and guiding candidates to choose their desirable paths and career options. We always refer students to the best universities and courses from the first conversation to the completion of their course. It is our main job to provide the best and most professional services always.

Does your institute provide any faculty support?

Yes. NIMTT has over 270+ empaneled faculty members across every department. At present, our online classes are ongoing, and we will start our offline classes shortly.

What documents will we get from the university?

Students will get a University student portal, online profile, Student I-Card, online results, marksheets, certificates, and Migration.

Can we do any verification to check the authenticity of these degrees?

NIMTT is one of the most trusted brands in India, working with universities that are UGC, AIU, BCI, PCI, AICTE, NCTE approved. So, there is no question regarding authenticity. All degrees are highly valid and authentic, accepted by Government, private sectors, and foreign institutions. If verification is needed, you can visit the university campus directly or use third-party verification by applying through verification forms and paying the requisite charges as notified on the university portal.

Do you provide educational loans?

Yes, NIMTT is affiliated with many NBFCs and government banks through which we offer educational loans to students with minimal norms and documentation.

Can INSData: get a foreign university degree by staying in India?

Yes, you can obtain a foreign degree by staying in India through NIMTT. We are an authorized empanelled partner offering degrees from certificates to DSc, D.Litt, and others.

Do you require formal qualifications for an Honorary Doctorate degree?

As per the format, this degree is awarded based on experience and expertise. However, we require a minimum of Higher Secondary (HS) or its equivalent pass to process the application to the university.

Do you provide Honorary Doctorate degrees from Indian universities?

Yes, we provide Honorary Doctorates from NIILM University, Haryana, and M.K. University, Gujarat.

Do you Offer Vocational Courses as per new Govt of India Guidelines ?

Yes, we provide more than 500 vocational Courses in different streams at very reasonable fee.
