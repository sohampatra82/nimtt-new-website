Franchise Business Opportunity

A franchise business is a strategic way to expand an existing brand by distributing goods and services through a licensing agreement. It involves a formal contract between the franchisor and the franchisee, where the franchisee is granted the right to use the brand name, business model, and support systems provided by the franchisor.

This model allows established brands to grow rapidly while offering entrepreneurs the advantage of operating under a trusted name with a proven business system.

Franchisee Opportunity for Autonomous Courses

  • Business Model: We offer a proven and successful business framework, complete with operational procedures, training programs, and marketing assistance.
  • Training & Support: Comprehensive training and ongoing support are provided to ensure high-quality service standards across all centers.
  • Franchise Fees & Royalties:
    • Universal Model: ₹25,000 (Non-refundable)
    • Premium Model: ₹50,000 (Non-refundable)
    • Includes royalty payments and other applicable charges (e.g., supplies, marketing).
  • Location: The franchise can operate from a commercial or semi-commercial property—leased or owned.
  • Space Requirements:
    • Universal Model: Minimum 1000 sq. ft.
    • Premium Model: Minimum 2000 sq. ft.
  • Profit Sharing: Franchisee retains 70% profit; Brand receives 30%.
  • Faculty Support: Provided by the brand if required.
  • Guest Lectures: Scheduled guest faculty sessions available at all centers for all models.
  • Placement Support: Offered for selected courses across all departments.
  • E-Library Access: Students can access a wide range of professional study materials online by enrolling in the e-library.
  • Study Materials: Provided in hard copy format for all courses.
  • Department Access:
    • Universal Model: Franchisee can choose any 2 departments from the following:
      1. Computer Software and Hardware
      2. Fashion Designing and Tailoring
      3. Beauty and Wellness
      4. Management and PR
      5. Vocational Courses
    • Premium Model: Access to all departments. Franchisee must independently arrange practical rooms as per model norms.
