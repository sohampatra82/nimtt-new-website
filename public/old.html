About Us

The National Institute of Management and Technical Training, NIMTT, was founded in 2004. We offer a wide range of courses in 20 different departments, including regular, online, and distant learning options. Our courses are conducted in Association with 24 prestigious Indian universities that are recognized by the UGC (University Grants Commission) of the Government of India, as well as three government boards. With 2600 courses available, students can choose from various levels of education, starting from Class X up to PhD, DSc, DLitt, and LLD. Whether you are looking to enhance your skills or pursue higher education, NIMTT provides a diverse range of options to suit your needs.

Read More

Our Associated University and Boards

We are proud to associate with 24 prestigious Indian universities that have been approved by various regulatory bodies such as AICTE, NCTE, BCI, PCI, NAAC, and other 3 government boards. These universities are renowned for their exceptional educational standards and have been recognized for their excellence in providing quality education. By working with these universities, we ensure that our students receive a world-class education that is recognized and respected both nationally and internationally. Our partnership with these universities allows us to offer a wide range of programs and courses that cater to diverse academic interests and career aspirations. With the support of these esteemed institutions, we strive to empower our students with the knowledge, skills, and qualifications necessary to succeed in their chosen fields.
