History

The Organisation NIMTT was at first known as NITT (National Institute of Technical Training) formed in the year 2004 in the month of June by a great visionist Mr. Samir Nath, an Electronics & Communication Engineer and a Management Professional and Ms. Nayna Nath , a General Graduate .With the brand NITT , it introduced around 30 jobs oriented courses with monopoly courseware and best industry demand syllabus. By the year end of 2005 the mission was to introduce some professional Management courses where both the visionist found that the name should have the word “management” within it and therefore the name suggested was “NIMTT” which was “NATIONAL INSTITITE OF MANAGEMENT AND TECHNICAL TRAINING” NIMTT was a Division formed under a resolution by the members of the Society named BAGHAJATIN MECHANICAL TRAINING EDUCATION SOCIETY, an Organization Regd. under the Govt. of West Bengal under Society Registration Act XXVI of 1860 in the year 2005. After the formation we have actively started to work in a) IT Education b) Management Education c) Corporate Training d) Software Development e) Web- Designing The Organisation has it Registered Office at 47 R.S.C Mallick Road , Jadavpur, Ground and Kolkata-700032. The Organisation addresses business opportunities in the retail training and the education segment with a thrust on long term career courses and the high end short term courses in IT, Multimedia and soft skill training. The Organisation also had a significant business at segment where it offered total training solutions to Govt , Corporate and working professionals

 

In the year 2006 the Management Body has thought to opt for quality policy and the Organisation has achieved the ISO 9001:2000 Certification. The Organisation has opened two branches in two places one in Kolkata city and the other in another state in Agartala in the state of Tripura

 

As we are always into development, the main members of the existing society, The President and The Vice-President came together and formed a Company named NIMTT COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY PVT LTD under Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India and Company Act of 1956 in the month of July 2008 . The Brand “NIMTT” is also Registered under Trade Marks Registration Act, Govt. of India in the year 2009 April

 

The Company has in house capability of developing new concepts and solutions. With more than 4years of experience in the field of IT NIMTT has fully independent Creative & Designing Team and also Programming team. In order to serve the latest and the best training solutions towards students the Company constantly upgrades themselves to the latest and the most appropriate technology available in the industry . NIMTT is always motivated by new innovations and upcoming technology and believes that the sky is the limit. Hence there is a constant endeavor to update and enhance the existing solution with respect to that of the latest and the best technology in the market.

 

NIMTT has been committed to creating a people driven organization since its inception and has built a very competent, stable and motivated core team of professionals. Every associate is a part of NIMTT Family and not just a faceless resource. Every member can expect to be treated as an individual with respect, dignity and integrity. NIMTT has created a management style that focuses on creating a sense of belonging, a feeling of Ownership and an Entrepreneurial attitude. NIMTT is also providing a well rounded experience to its professionals that include acquisition of business domain knowledge .

 

The company is very fortunate to have highly educated and experienced personalities, the Board of Directors as well as the Board of Advisors who understand the necessity and the demand of the today’s market and guides us in development
